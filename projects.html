PROJECTS

Google Earth Project

During the fall of 2017, as a research assistant at the University of St.Thomas, I was assigned the Google Earth Project. This project was based on the article, Mapping public and private spaces of urban agriculture in Chicago through the analysis of high-resolution aerial images in Google Earth, by John R. Taylor & Sarah Taylor Lovell. The end goal of this project was to map all possible private and public gardens in St. Paul's watershed region on Google Earth. Throughout a few months staring at the computer, I pinned down all the gardens that were in shaded region. The gardens I found consisted of large community gardens and small gardens from different homes, which I had to zoom in each house so I wouldn't miss any. Thus, the final result is on the screenshot.

Accounting Project

In my introduction to financial accounting course, we were assigned into groups for the Financial Statement Analysis Project. The company we were given to do an analysis was Target Corporation. The purpose of this project was to provide an analysis of Target's financial statement by collecting data on Target's financial condition, operating results, cash flows and capital structure from the Form 10-K. My analysis was focused on specialize to market. The sales were increasing by a little bit during the pandemic. Some recommendations I came up with in order to increase sales were to tailor to different geographic region by adding more ethnic foods and collaborate with new brands and influencers.

Music Project

A few weeks ago, we were assigned to mix a song that the proffessor is about to release. In the Arts of Mixing course, we are currently learning about different techniques to mix a song in order for it to be ready for its release. The purpose of these projects is to apply the mixing techniques we learn in class to either others or our own songs in order to sound the best it can across streaming platforms. In this project, I've added an equalizer to dial out frequencies I don't want from certain sounds, added a compressor to reduce dynamics of a sound, and added reverb to give the mix warmth and space.
